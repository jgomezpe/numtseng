Many of the happy moments we have in life are those when we learn something, for example:
  • When we learn to walk, to ride a bike, or to drive a car.
  • When we learn to paint, to play a musical instrument, or to practice some sport.
  • When we learn a language and can use it comunicate our ideas.
  • When we learn new methods for solving our problems.

Learn, allows us to adquire konwledge about things around us and to gain hability in activities we can carry on. When we learn, such knowledge and habilities are internalized, that means, those become part of us.

For learning, we must be ready to:
  • Study: Pay attention, in a very detail and interested fashion, to 'things' that are present in our reality.
  • Experience: Interact with our reality, be ready to try, to make mistakes, to correct ourselves, to try as many times until we make it.
  • Be guided: Allow people that already learnt to share their expiriences and travel along us the path we want to walk.
