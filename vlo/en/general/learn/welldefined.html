While we are learning, it is possible to find things that are well defined, that means, we will be able to say what they are in a precise manner, but sometimes we will find things that are not well defined or may be we can only say what can we do with them or what those things can do. We will call fundamental things to things that are not well defined while we will call derivate things to those things that are well defined, regardless if such definition considers fundamental things.

Sometimes, with the idea of making more simple our learning process, we propose naive definition for fundamental things. Such naive definitions take in consideration our intuitive understanding about things.
